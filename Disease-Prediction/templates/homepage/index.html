Take charge of your own health...

There may have been numerous occasions when you or someone close to you needed immediate medical assistance, but a doctor wasn't available due to various reasons. The Disease Prediction system is designed to provide end-user support and online consultation. In this proposal, we introduce a web application that enables users to receive instant guidance regarding their health conditions through an intelligent system. The application is equipped with a database of various details and the diseases associated with them. Users can share their health concerns through the application, which then processes the specific details to identify potential illnesses associated with them. Intelligent data mining techniques are employed to make accurate predictions about the likely illness based on the user's information.
